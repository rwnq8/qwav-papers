Autaxys: A Unified Process Ontology of Reality

1. Introduction: The Imperative for a New Foundational Principle

1.1. Limitations of Current Ontological Frameworks

Current scientific and philosophical frameworks, predominantly substance-oriented, struggle to provide a comprehensive, unified understanding of reality. This “noun/verb confusion” often treats fundamental particles and laws as axiomatic, rather than emergent properties of a deeper generative process. Concepts like “information” or “logos” frequently lack the precision to describe the intrinsic source of complexity and order. These frameworks face significant challenges in explaining emergent complexity, intrinsic order, and, critically, in unifying General Relativity and Quantum Mechanics. A pressing need exists for a deeper, generative explanation of existence, one that moves beyond static descriptions to a dynamic, process-oriented view.

1.2. The Need for a Generative, Pattern-Based Ontology

A new foundational principle is required, shifting focus from static “things” to dynamic processes and emergent patterns. This ontology must emphasize the intrinsic source of complexity and structure, focusing on the genesis and inherent unfolding of order rather than merely describing its manifestations. Reality, in this view, is not a collection of objects but a continuous act of becoming—a self-generating and self-organizing system.

1.3. Introducing Autaxys and Autology

To address these limitations, the Autaxys Framework introduces two core concepts:

  • Autaxys: Derived from the Greek auto- (self) and taxis (order/arrangement), Autaxys is defined as the principle of intrinsic self-ordering, self-arranging, and self-generating patterned existence. It denotes a naturalistic, immanent, and self-generating principle that underlies all reality, operating without reliance on external forces or pre-existing conditions. Autaxys is the generative process itself.

  • Autology: This is the interdisciplinary field dedicated to the systematic study of Autaxys. Autology aims to provide a “new way of seeing” reality, emphasizing generative principles, the primacy of pattern over substance, and the intrinsic coherence of the universe. It seeks to understand how reality continuously creates and sustains itself through inherent processes.

2. Autaxys Defined: The Principle of Intrinsic Self-Generation and Patterned Order

2.1. Formal Definition of Autaxys and the Cosmic Algorithm

Autaxys posits that reality is a self-ordering, self-arranging, and self-generating system, fundamentally an evolving algorithm. This “cosmic algorithm” is a “simultaneous functional equation of existence” where near-infinite interdependent variables continuously generate and sustain reality’s output. Operating without external reliance, Autaxys is the continuous execution and evolution of this self-modifying program, representing the ultimate “Bit” from which all “Its” (observable phenomena) derive.

2.2. Fundamental Principles of Autaxys: The Intrinsic Logic of Reality

Autaxys operates according to inherent principles that define its self-organization and guide the universe’s unfolding. These principles are not external laws, but intrinsic properties of the generative process itself:

  • Ontological Primacy: Autaxys is the ultimate ground of being, the uncaused cause, and the irreducible source of all existence. It is fundamentally an autopoietic (self-producing) system, requiring no external cause for its existence or operation.
  • Dynamic and Processual Nature: Reality is in a constant state of becoming, fundamentally “verb-centric,” characterized by continuous transformation and emergence.
  • Intrinsic Coherence (Universal Self-Consistency): All patterns and processes within reality must be self-consistent and harmoniously integrated. This principle mandates a universal self-consistency.
  • Conservation of Distinguishability (Ontological Inertia of Pattern): Fundamental distinguishability, once established, tends to be conserved, providing an ontological inertia to patterns.
  • Parsimony in Generative Mechanisms (Intrinsic Elegance): Reality operates with the minimal set of rules necessary to achieve maximal generative output, leading to inherent elegance in its structure.
  • Intrinsic Determinacy and Emergent Probabilism (Autaxic Causality): Every pattern and transformation is a necessary consequence of a prior state within the Autaxic algorithm. Apparent probabilism arises from the computational complexity of the Adjudication stage, where multiple possibilities are evaluated.
  • Interactive Complexity Maximization (Drive Towards Richness, Constrained by Stability): Autaxys continuously explores and actualizes increasing levels of complexity, but this drive is always constrained by the requirements of stability and coherence, ensuring that complexity is sustainable.
  • Pattern-Generating Capacity: The primary mode of manifestation for Autaxys is the generation of patterns, from the most fundamental to the most complex.
  • Foundation for Information: Information, in this framework, is not primary but arises derivatively from the distinctions and arrangements inherent in the patterns generated by Autaxys.
  • Self-Articulation: Reality expresses and describes itself through its own generative activity, continuously unfolding its inherent properties.

3. The Universal Relational Graph (URG): The Substrate of Reality

The Universal Relational Graph (URG) serves as the dynamic, computational medium upon which Autaxys operates. It is not a static physical space, but a constantly evolving network of relationships.

  • Axiomatic Qualia: The URG is built upon a finite, fixed alphabet of fundamental, irreducible properties, referred to as Axiomatic Qualia (e.g., spin, charge, flavor). These are the most basic, indivisible distinctions from which all other complexity arises.
  • Distinctions (Nodes): These are unique instances of co-occurring Qualia, representing discrete events or points of reality within the URG. They are the fundamental “bits” of existence.
  • Relations (Edges): These are emergent properties that link Distinctions, representing dynamic interactions, interdependencies, and causal connections. The URG is characterized by complex feedback loops and mutual dependencies between its nodes and edges, constantly reconfiguring itself.

4. The Autaxic Trilemma: The Core Generative Engine

4.1. The Trilemma Defined: Novelty, Efficiency, Persistence (N, E, P)

The Autaxic Trilemma is the fundamental, inescapable paradox driving Autaxys. It represents a constant negotiation between three locally competitive but globally synergistic imperatives: Novelty (N), Efficiency (E), and Persistence (P). This dynamic interplay gives rise to the laws of physics as stable, emergent solutions. The Trilemma is governed by an objective function, the Autaxic Lagrangian (L_A), defined as:

L_A = N(G) × E(G) × P(G) (where G is the URG)

  • Novelty (N(G)): This imperative drives the generation of new distinctions, patterns, and configurations. It is the source of mass-energy, cosmic expansion, and irreducible information, pushing the universe towards exploration and complexity.
  • Efficiency (E(G)): This imperative optimizes resource utilization, minimizes redundancy, and maximizes algorithmic compressibility. It drives structural elegance, symmetries, and conservation laws, promoting streamlined and economical operations.
  • Persistence (P(G)): This imperative maintains existing patterns, ensures causal integrity, and resists dissolution. It drives causality, stability, the arrow of time, and structural resilience, ensuring the continuity of established forms.

4.2. The Generative Cycle: Iterative Actualization of Reality

The universe evolves through discrete, iterative t → t+1 transformations of its universal state, representing a fundamental “quantum of change” or “cosmic clock cycle.” This cycle describes how the cosmic algorithm “creates other algorithms” or “evolves as it executes,” leading to an “evolving set of equations” (effective rules of physics). This implies a form of cosmic learning or adaptation, where the universe refines its “code” based on the outcomes of each cycle. The future emerges from this ongoing, dynamic, and potentially novel algorithmic process.

4.2.1. Stage 1: PROLIFERATION (Novelty & Exploration)

This stage involves the unconstrained execution of Exploration Operators (EMERGE, BIND, TRANSFORM), creating a superposition of possible new URG states (analogous to the universal wave function). It is a phase of maximal potentiality and exploration, driven by the Novelty imperative.

4.2.2. Stage 2: ADJUDICATION (Efficiency & Selection)

A global computation of the Autaxic Lagrangian (L_A) occurs across all proposed states. Each state is assigned a coherence score, defining a probability landscape (a “reality amplifier”). The Selection Operator (RESOLVE) implicitly evaluates and prioritizes states. The apparent probabilism observed in quantum mechanics is an emergent property of the computational complexity involved in this arbitration, driven by the Efficiency imperative.

4.2.3. Stage 3: SOLIDIFICATION (Persistence & Enforcing Reality)

The chosen optimal state(s) (those with the highest L_A score) are irreversibly actualized, while unselected configurations are purged. This stage intrinsically links to the increase of thermodynamic entropy and the establishment of the causal arrow of time, driven by the Persistence imperative. The RESOLVE operator explicitly actualizes these states.

4.3. Autaxic Mechanisms: Operators and Dynamics

The Autaxic Trilemma and Generative Cycle are governed by a set of fundamental operators and core operational dynamics that define the “how” of reality’s self-generation.

4.3.1. Generative Operators: The Syntax of Change

These are the fundamental actions that transform the URG, directly employed within the Generative Cycle: * EMERGE: Creates new distinctions (nodes) or relations (edges) within the URG, introducing novelty. * BIND: Forms stable, persistent connections between distinctions, promoting coherence and structure. * TRANSFORM: Modifies existing distinctions or relations, allowing for evolution and change. * RESOLVE: Selects the most coherent and stable configurations from the proposed variations, actualizing them into reality. Physical laws are understood as the fundamental syntax of these operators, defining what is possible and prohibited within the Autaxic process. Statistical laws emerge from the probabilistic outcomes of the RESOLVE operator.

4.3.2. Core Operational Dynamics: The Verbs of Creation

These describe the continuous creative activity of Autaxys, manifesting through the operators: 1. Relational Processing: The continuous creation, propagation, interaction, and transformation of distinctions and relations within the URG. 2. Symmetry Realization and Spontaneous Symmetry Breaking (SSB): The genesis of forms and structures, where higher symmetries give rise to lower, more specific ones, shaping the universe’s architecture. 3. Feedback Dynamics (Amplification and Damping): The sculpting of stability and complexity through positive feedback (amplifying patterns) and negative feedback (damping fluctuations and clamping states). 4. Resonance and Coherence Establishment: The basis for harmony and integrated structures, where patterns amplify and synchronize, leading to stable configurations. 5. Critical State Transitions and Emergent Hierarchies: The mechanism by which the architecture of complexity evolves, leading to qualitative transformations and the emergence of new levels of organization.

4.4. Synergy and Self-Tuning: The Coherently Functioning Unified System

The Autaxys Framework describes a deeply interconnected, synergistic system where fundamental principles (the grammar) and dynamics (the verbs) work in concert, driven by the Trilemma and actualized through the Generative Cycle. This continuous unfolding of reality from undifferentiated potentiality offers a novel solution to the universe’s “fine-tuning” problem, suggesting Autaxys inherently “tunes itself” through its ongoing generative process.

5. Emergent Physics: From Algorithm to Cosmos

Within the Autaxys Framework, every observable physical property manifests an underlying computational characteristic. This Principle of Computational Equivalence posits that information—patterned distinctions and relationships within the URG—is ontologically primary, and physical reality is its emergent expression. The universe is a continuously executing and evolving algorithm.

5.1. The Emergent Arena: Spacetime, Gravity, and the Vacuum

  • Spacetime: Emerges as a causal data structure from the Generative Cycles and evolving URG relations. Distance and time are measures of relational complexity and the number of processing steps within the URG.
  • Gravity: An emergent phenomenon resulting from the URG’s intrinsic tendency to reconfigure itself to ascend the L_A coherence gradient. Mass-energy, as highly persistent and novel patterns, locally warps the URG’s processing landscape.
  • The Vacuum: Represents the default, maximally dynamic state of the Generative Cycle, characterized by a maximal flux of virtual patterns. This corresponds to the “zero-point energy” of the URG, a sea of potentiality.

5.2. The Emergent Actors: Particles, Mass, and Charge

  • Particles: Stable, self-reinforcing patterns of relational complexity within the URG. They are persistent, localized dynamic processes, akin to standing waves or resonant states within the universal computational field.
  • Mass-Energy: Represents the physical “cost” of information. It is a measure of a pattern’s inherent Novelty (its informational incompressibility) and its Persistence (its stability within the URG).
  • Conserved Quantities (Charge): Are physical expressions of deep symmetries and efficiencies that are highly favored by the Autaxic Trilemma, representing stable configurations within the URG.

5.3. The Constants of the Algorithm

Fundamental physical constants are interpreted as intrinsic parameters of the Autaxic Generative Cycle: * Speed of Light (c): Represents the maximum rate at which relational links can be traversed or information can propagate within the URG, essentially the URG’s processing speed. * Planck’s Constant (h/ħ): Represents the fundamental unit of change, the minimum “cost” or quantum of action required for one complete Generative Cycle.

5.4. Cosmic-Scale Phenomena: Dark Matter & Dark Energy

  • Dark Energy: Interpreted as the cosmic manifestation of the Novelty imperative, an intrinsic drive within Autaxys to expand and explore new configurations, leading to the universe’s accelerated metric expansion.
  • Dark Matter: Represents stable, high-Persistence patterns within the URG that are gravitationally active but computationally “shy,” meaning they do not interact via other forces in the same way as ordinary matter.

5.5. Computational Inertia and the Emergence of the Classical World

  • The Quantum Realm as the Native State: The quantum realm is the direct expression of the Generative Cycle in its raw, fundamental state, where the Trilemma is actively arbitrated and possibilities explored.
  • The Classical Limit: This is an emergent threshold effect where high Persistence leads to “computational inertia.” Patterns become highly stable, deterministic, and predictable, giving rise to the classical world.
  • Entanglement: Is a direct computational artifact of the Adjudication stage, where shared non-local selection rules are enforced by the global L_A computation, leading to instantaneous correlations between distant particles.

6. The Mass-Frequency Identity: Unifying Relativity and Quantum Mechanics

The Autaxys Framework offers a profound unification of Einstein’s E=mc² and Planck’s E=hν through the bridge equation, establishing frequency as a universal principle underlying all physical phenomena:

\(ħω_C = m_0c²\)

Here, ħ (reduced Planck’s constant) and c (speed of light) are understood as intrinsic scaling parameters of the fundamental dynamic medium (URG) and the Autaxic process itself. In natural units (\(ħ=1\), \(c=1\)), this simplifies to \(m_0 = ω_C\), meaning rest mass is equivalent to an intrinsic processing frequency or Compton frequency.

6.1. Physical Interpretation: Mass as a Resonant State of Fundamental Patterns

Reality is fundamentally a self-generating, self-organizing process (Autaxys) operating on a universal computational field (URG). All phenomena, including particles, are characterized by intrinsic processing frequencies. * Resonance, Stability, and Pattern Hierarchy: Elementary particles are stable standing waves or resonant patterns within the URG. The Compton frequency (ω_C) represents their fundamental oscillation, and the particle mass hierarchy is a discrete spectrum of allowed resonant frequencies. This stability is a direct outcome of the Persistence imperative of the Autaxic Trilemma. * The Dynamic Substrate: Quantum fields are the dynamic fabric of the URG, and zero-point energy is a manifestation of its inherent dynamism. * Higgs Mechanism Reinterpretation: The Higgs mechanism, in this context, determines a pattern’s “impedance” or resistance to oscillation within the URG, promoting its solidification and persistence, thereby defining its intrinsic frequency (mass). This aligns with the Efficiency imperative, optimizing the pattern’s stable integration into the URG.

6.2. Information-Theoretic Framework: Mass as Complexity, Frequency as Tempo

  • Mass as Informational Complexity: Rest mass is interpreted as a pattern’s inherent informational or algorithmic complexity. Higher mass implies a more intricate, incompressible pattern that requires a higher tempo of processing to maintain. This directly relates to the Novelty imperative, as novel patterns introduce new information.
  • Frequency as Operational Tempo: The intrinsic frequency (Compton frequency) of a pattern is its fundamental processing rate or “clock speed” within the URG. It represents the continuous regeneration, validation, and maintenance of the pattern’s structure.
  • Resonance (Physical Stability) ↔︎ Self-Validation (Informational Coherence): A stable physical pattern is a resonant state, harmonically sustained by Autaxys. This physical stability is equivalent to the pattern’s informational self-consistency and coherence within the URG, reflecting the Principle of Intrinsic Coherence. This leads to a fundamental law of cosmic computation: a pattern’s operational tempo is proportional to its informational complexity, and its stability measures its self-validation.

6.3. Interactions and Emergent Phenomena

  • Reinterpreting Fundamental Forces: Fundamental forces are mechanisms that alter patterns’ resonant states or transfer modulatory information between them, acting like “tuning forks” that influence other patterns’ frequencies within the URG.
  • Reinterpreting Gravity and Spacetime: Spacetime is a dynamic manifestation of the URG’s evolving relational structure. Gravity arises from high-frequency (massive) patterns locally altering the surrounding URG’s intrinsic processing frequencies, creating a gradient that other patterns follow.
